My Inspiration

It all started when I was in high school. On Youtube, I happened to come across a guitar video of Sungha Jung. Just after a few seconds of watching, I was amazed at how talented this guy was. In the video he was around 9 - 10 years old! I was really inspired after watching a couple of his videos. I was able to convince my parents to buy me a guitar. From there, I took a few lessons and then started learning fingerstyle on my own.

At First, It was very hard to learn guitar. For the acoustic guitar, the strings are made of steel so It hurts your fingers after a while. Another frustrating part was that some fingering was hard, such as bar chords. For these chords, your pointer finger would have to play more than 1 string. As the result, I took a long break from guitar. I didn't quit, but I only learned songs once in a while.

Recently, I discovered one of my closest friends also played fingerstyle guitar. This made me happy and also inspired me to play more guitar. I try to play every day now. One day we hope to play a duet.
